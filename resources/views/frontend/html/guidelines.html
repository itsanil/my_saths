Guidelines for fundraisers

DO'S


  • Do share correct and genuine personal information such as full name, email , registered address.
  • Do create your own campaign and source your own content.
  • Do ensure that the information in fundraisers created by you do not infringe upon the intellectual property, trade secret or other proprietary rights or rights of publicity or privacy rights of third parties. Like the images you use, video you use, make sure you have permission.
  • Do have a marketing and branding strategy in place.
  • Do read the terms of use and Privacy policy carefully before starting a campaign.
  • Do intimate us if you feel your account has been compromised


DON'Ts


  • Do not add the following as rewards and perks :
  • Capital, equity, or other ownership interest in a company or venture.
  • Financial incentive
  • Any alcoholic consumer product
  • Drugs and any other controlled substance.
  • Weapons, ammunition and related accessories.
  • Any form of lottery or gambling.
  • Any form or air transportation
  • Do not use inappropriate content in your campaign, like porn or other indecent videos, images, offensive, illegal, violate the rights of, harm, or threaten the safety of any person. If you do your campaign will ease to exist.
  • Do not associate yourself, directly or indirectly with any other crowdfunding website, while you are crowdfunding on OnlineSensor for the same project and cause.
  • Do not make illegal and/or unauthorized use of OnlineSensor's services, including collecting usernames and/or email addresses of other users by electronic or other means for the purpose of sending unsolicited email or unauthorized framing of this Website, is prohibited
